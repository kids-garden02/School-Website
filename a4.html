Republic Day Commemoration — Honoring Our Nation

With great pride and patriotic spirit, Kids Garden Sr. Sec. School, Pilani marked the 76th Republic Day through a series of meaningful activities and performances. The ceremony began with flag hoisting, followed by a well-choreographed line-up of cultural performances by our talented students.

Highlights included patriotic skits, speeches, and dance performances, all echoing the values of unity, freedom, and national pride. These events exemplify our belief that the heart of education lies in instilling strong moral and civic values in every learner.

Our Republic Day celebration garnered praise from parents and educators alike, reinforcing our reputation among the best schools in Pilani and further cementing our place in the top 10 schools in Pilani.

As a forward-thinking Pilani school, we ensure that every occasion contributes to the overall development of our students—not just intellectually, but emotionally and ethically.
